Dr.Magdi Yacoub

Dr. Magdi Yacoub is one of the most renowned and respected heart surgeons in the world. Born on November 16, 1935, in Bilbeis, Egypt, he is often referred to as a pioneer in cardiac surgery and has made groundbreaking contributions to the field of cardiology and heart transplantation. His work has saved countless lives and earned him international recognition and numerous awards.

Elezaby Pharmacy

El Ezaby Pharmacies is recognized as Egypt’s uncontested leading provider of health and personal care products. A success story that began with one pharmacy established in 1975, the group is now a major corporation driving the country’s healthcare sector. With nearly 40 years of experience serving the Egyptian community, El Ezaby Pharmacies has an ambitious plan to cement its leadership across the region.

Seif Pharmacy

Seif Pharmacy is a trusted Egyptian pharmacy chain offering a wide range of medications, health products, and medical services. With numerous branches, including 24/7 locations, it provides convenient access to expert consultations, medication advice, and health programs like free check-ups and vaccination drives. It also offers online ordering, home delivery, and telepharmacy services. Committed to quality and community health, Seif Pharmacy engages in health awareness campaigns and partnerships, making it a reliable healthcare provider in Egypt.
