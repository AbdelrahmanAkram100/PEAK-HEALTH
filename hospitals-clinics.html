Cleopatra Hospitals Group

Cleopatra Hospital, part of the Cleopatra Hospitals Group (CHG), is a leading private healthcare provider in Egypt, renowned for its high-quality medical services and state-of-the-art facilities. Established in 1984 in Cairo, it has grown into the largest private healthcare network in the country, offering specialized care in cardiology, oncology, orthopedics, fertility, and more, through centers of excellence like the Cleopatra Heart Center and Cleopatra Oncology Center. Accredited by international bodies such as the Joint Commission International (JCI), the hospital is equipped with advanced technology, including robotic surgery and diagnostic imaging, and staffed by highly trained medical professionals. CHG, the first private healthcare group listed on the Egyptian Stock Exchange, continues to expand its services, focusing on patient-centric care, community health initiatives, and medical tourism, solidifying its reputation as a trusted name in Egypt’s healthcare sector.

Saudi German Hospital

Saudi German Hospital (SGH) is one of the leading private healthcare providers in the Middle East, known for its high-quality medical services and state-of-the-art facilities. Part of the Saudi German Health Group, the hospital was established in 1988 in Jeddah, Saudi Arabia, and has since expanded to become a network of hospitals across the Middle East, including branches in Dubai, Cairo, Riyadh, and Aseer. SGH is renowned for its patient-centric approach, offering a wide range of medical services, including specialized care in cardiology, oncology, orthopedics, neurology, and women’s health, as well as advanced surgical procedures and emergency care.

Dar Al Fouad Hospital

Dar Al Fouad Hospital, founded in 1999, is one of Egypt’s leading healthcare institutions, offering world-class medical services across multiple specialties, including cardiology, oncology, neurology, nephrology, orthopedics, and organ transplantation. It has performed over 600 liver transplants and 250 bone marrow transplants, making it a leader in these fields in the Middle East and Africa. The hospital has affiliations with the Cleveland Clinic Foundation (USA) and holds Joint Commission International (JCI) accreditation, ensuring adherence to global healthcare standards. Its main branch is in 6th of October City, with a second state-of-the-art facility in Nasr City, opened in 2018 after a $120 million investment, offering advanced medical units for radiotherapy, chemotherapy, and specialized short-stay treatments. With a strong commitment to patient-centered care, Dar Al Fouad provides dedicated services for international patients, assisting with airport transfers, accommodation, and medical tourism needs.

As-Salam International Hospital

As-Salam International Hospital, established in 1982, is a premier tertiary care facility in Cairo, Egypt, and a key part of the Alameda Healthcare Group. Accredited by the Joint Commission International (JCI) and certified with ISO 9001 and ISO 14001, the hospital is committed to providing high-quality healthcare while maintaining strong environmental and safety standards. With over 700 physicians and 400 nurses, As-Salam offers advanced medical services across 31 specialties, including cardiology, oncology, neurology, nephrology, and orthopedics. It is also known for its renowned Liver and Kidney Transplant Unit and Renal Dialysis Unit, making it a leader in organ transplantation and kidney care in Egypt. The hospital operates multiple branches, including a state-of-the-art facility in New Cairo, ensuring accessibility to high-quality medical care. It maintains an affiliation with the Royal Free Hospital in London, enhancing its expertise and ensuring international medical standards.

57357 Hospital

57357 Hospital, also known as the Children's Cancer Hospital Egypt (CCHE), is a world-renowned, non-profit pediatric oncology hospital in Cairo, Egypt, providing free, high-quality cancer treatment to children. Equipped with advanced technology and following international protocols, it offers comprehensive care, including chemotherapy, radiation therapy, and bone marrow transplantation, alongside psychological, social, and educational support. Renowned for its high cure rates and holistic approach, the hospital relies on donations and community support to sustain its operations. Since its establishment in 2007, it has become a global model for healthcare excellence, combining cutting-edge treatment, research, and education to transform pediatric cancer care in the region and beyond.

International Medical Center

The International Medical Center (IMC) in El Shorouk City, Cairo, Egypt, is a leading tertiary care hospital established in 2005 through collaboration between the Egyptian Armed Forces and the U.S. Army Corps of Engineers. It provides advanced medical and surgical services across multiple specialties, serving both military personnel and civilians. Accredited by the Joint Commission International (JCI), IMC upholds global healthcare standards and features state-of-the-art medical equipment, intensive care units, and a rehabilitation center. The facility also includes a hotel section for patients' families and international visitors. Located on the Cairo-Ismailia Desert Road (km 42), it plays a crucial role in Egypt’s healthcare sector.
